Serving
Clean. Green. Ethical.

Ensuring an everlasting smile on the farmer's face by
promoting sustainable, climate resilient, and ethical farming.

Ensuring Farmer's Sustainability, Climate Resilience and Consumer Satisfaction

At Balaghat Farms, we believe that sustainable, ethical farming and agricultural practices today, can ensure assured productivity for a growing population in the future.

We make sure that farming is not just environmentally green, but also sustainable for the producer, bringing assured income. Our value pillars "CLEAN, GREEN, ETHICAL" root from climate resilient farming and economical farming practices.

Milestones

Established

December 2020

Formed in December 2020, admist a pandemic, after a decade long intensive study, with a vision to lead small land holding farmers in building a sustainable business and life.

INCUBATION

January 2021

Incubated as one of India's 15 most promising Farmer producer organization(FPO's) at HortiSquare(H-Square)
Balaghat Farms is included in a cohort that has India's most promising FPO and FPC for an incubation programme of 3 years

ASSOCIATION

January 2021

Association with Sahyadri Farms Sustainable Grassroots Initiative(SFSGIL) has empowered 99 families, in the Balaghat region, by providing them a better financial return than the market.
